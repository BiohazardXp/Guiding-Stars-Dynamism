# ⚡ Quick Reference: What You Can & Can't Change Through CMS

## 🟢 YES - Fully Editable Through CMS (No Code Needed)

### Text Content:
- ✅ Hero title & subtitle (Home page top banner)
- ✅ About descriptions (Home page about section)
- ✅ Testimonial names, titles, quotes (all 10 slots)
- ✅ Footer company info & contact details
- ✅ Footer link labels and social media URLs
- ✅ Page descriptions and introductions

### Images:
- ✅ Upload and manage testimonial photos
- ✅ Upload images for footer or other content
- ✅ Replace images by uploading new ones with same name

### Organization:
- ✅ Create/edit/delete content items
- ✅ Choose which page each content appears on
- ✅ Organize content by section (Hero, About, Footer, etc.)

│  └─ testimonials_cta

ABOUT PAGE
├─ Content .......................... 0% (Code only)

TEAM PAGE
├─ Team Members ..................... 0% (Code only)

CONTACT PAGE

└─ Footer ........................... 100% (CMS)

GRADUATION PAGE
├─ Content .......................... 0% (Code only)

ALL PAGES
